Our Chicken Dishes

Fried Chicken

Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside—this golden fried chicken delivers the perfect crunch with every bite, seasoned to perfection for an all-time favorite.

Chicken Inasal

A smoky, grilled Filipino favorite where chicken is marinated in a zesty blend of lemongrass, calamansi, and annatto, served with a delicious char and packed with flavor.

Chicken Adobo

A Filipino classic of marinated chicken cooked in a tangy blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, delivering a bold, flavorful dish with every bite.

Chicken Curry

A hearty, aromatic dish where tender chicken is simmered in a rich coconut milk sauce infused with spices like turmeric and curry powder, offering a flavorful and warming experience.

Chicken Hamonado

A sweet and savory Filipino dish where tender chicken is slow-cooked in a luscious pineapple sauce, balancing rich flavors with a touch of tropical sweetness.

Creamy Mushroom chicken

Juicy chicken smothered in a rich, velvety mushroom sauce, combining earthy flavors with a creamy finish for an irresistible meal.

Tinolang Manok

A comforting Filipino soup featuring tender chicken, ginger, green papaya, and leafy greens, simmered in a savory broth that's perfect for rainy days.
